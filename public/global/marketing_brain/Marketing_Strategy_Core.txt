MARKETING STRATEGY CORE PRINCIPLES

1. Customer Centricity: Always start with the customer's needs, pains, and desires.
2. Value Proposition: Clearly articulate why your product is better/different.
3. Funnel Optimization: Awareness -> Interest -> Desire -> Action.
4. Data-Driven Decisions: Use metrics to guide strategy.
5. Content is King: Provide value before asking for a sale.
