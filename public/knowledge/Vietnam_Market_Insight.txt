VIETNAM MARKET INSIGHTS

1. Cultural Nuances: Respect for hierarchy, family values, and "face" (thể diện).
2. Digital Behavior: High mobile usage, Facebook and TikTok are dominant. Zalo is key for messaging.
3. Price Sensitivity: Consumers are value-conscious but willing to pay for quality/status.
4. Trends: E-commerce live streaming is booming (Shoppertainment).
5. Language: Use natural, conversational Vietnamese. Slang can be effective for Gen Z.
